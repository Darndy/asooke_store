About Us

Welcome to AJOKE ALASOOKE STORE

As a developer, my passion for both technology and culture inspired me to create a platform that not only showcases the beauty of our local fabrics but also celebrates the artistry behind traditional practices like gele styling. I saw an opportunity to merge modern e-commerce with the rich heritage of our fashion, offering a space where people can easily access unique fabrics and expert services. By building this website, I aim to preserve and promote our traditions while embracing the digital age. Project Timeline Concept & Planning (Week 1): Defined the project scope, including the integration of e-commerce functionalities and the services section for gele styling. Design & Development (Week 1-3): Created wireframes and designed the website layout. Developed the website using Django, SQLite, and Bootstrap. Testing & Refinement (Month 1): Conducted thorough testing to ensure all features worked seamlessly, including user feedback for refinements. Launch & Deployment (Month 1-2): Successfully deployed the website and made it live, allowing users to explore and interact with the platform. This project is also part of my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I focus on developing innovative solutions and enhancing user experiences. Our Mission

We aim to celebrate and preserve the beauty of our cultural heritage through the sale of premium local fabrics and expert gele styling services. We are committed to offering high-quality products and services that reflect our rich traditions, while embracing modern technology to create a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers.
